Pokébase is a free and open-source Pokémon companion app that lets you explore the world of Pokémon and Trading Card Game (TCG) cards.

Features:
• Browse the full Pokédex with beautiful cards showing types, stats, and images
• Search and filter Pokémon by name in real time
• Explore Pokémon TCG cards with high-quality artwork
• Save card images to your photo library
• Offline caching — data loads instantly after the first visit
• Dark and light theme support
• Smooth animations and optimized performance

Pokébase uses only free, open data sources:
• PokéAPI for Pokémon data
• TCGdex for Trading Card Game data

No accounts, no tracking, no ads. Just Pokémon.
