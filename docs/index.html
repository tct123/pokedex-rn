Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 4, 2026

Introduction

Pokebase ("the App") is a free, open-source Pokédex application. Your privacy is important to us, and this policy explains how the App handles your information.

Data Collection

Pokebase does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected (name, email, location, etc.).
  • No analytics or tracking tools are used.
  • No cookies are set.
  • No accounts or sign-ups are required.

Network Usage

The App connects to the PokéAPI to fetch Pokémon data. These requests do not include any personal information.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party services that collect user data.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the "Last updated" date at the top will be revised. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of any changes.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
